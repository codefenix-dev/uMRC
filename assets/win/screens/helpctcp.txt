|09__[ |15How to use CTCP commands|07: |09]_______________________________|07

 CTCP (Client-to-Client Protocol) commands are special messages
 that can be sent to a channel or other clients.

 Usage:
   |15/|11ctcp |15target |09command|07

 A |15target |07is a user or room name. |09* |07targets all.

 Supported commands:
|09   VERSION  TIME  PING  CLIENTINFO|07
